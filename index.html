about

When I was younger, I would stay at my grandparent's house in Vancouver, BC over summer vacation. My grandpa had some trees on his property he wanted cut down, so I would, piece by piece, chop one tree down every summer. I would wake up with the sun, eat my Cinnamon Toast Crunch, and head outside. I would start hacking away only stopping to watch The Big Comfy Couch or Goosebumps and eat. This would happen every day until that tree came down. Nothing could keep me away from it. Being outside just felt right.

I loved everything about it. The smell, the sounds, the feeling. A bond formed that will resonate with me for life.

There's a beauty in nature that's hard to describe. Every living thing on Earth was created, more or less, through happenstance. Many things had to be just right for the world around us to exist as it is. Well, programming is much like the forces of nature. Piece by piece we create incredible things that would otherwise seem impossible. And now, every time I start clacking away, I'm reminded of the time where everything felt just right.
